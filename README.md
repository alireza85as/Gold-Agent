# Gold Analysis Agent 🪙🤖

An AI-powered Gold Market Assistant designed to analyze global gold markets, monitor price movements, understand market trends, and provide data-driven insights using real-time market information.

---

## Overview

Gold Analysis Agent is an intelligent conversational agent focused on gold market analysis (XAU/USD).

The agent combines market data, economic indicators, news analysis, and conversation memory to provide structured insights about gold prices, trends, and possible market scenarios.

The goal of this project is to build a professional AI market assistant capable of understanding financial market context while keeping responses accurate, structured, and data-driven.

---

## Features

### 📊 Real-Time Market Analysis

- Gold price tracking (XAU/USD)
- Local currency conversion (IRR)
- Market context injection
- Current market condition analysis


### 📈 Trend & Forecast Analysis

The agent can analyze:

- Market direction
- Bullish and bearish scenarios
- Factors affecting gold prices
- Possible future market conditions

Forecasts are generated as scenarios based on available data, not guaranteed predictions.


### 📰 News Intelligence

The system integrates market news to understand:

- Economic events
- Geopolitical risks
- Federal Reserve decisions
- Inflation impacts
- Global market sentiment


### 🧠 Conversation Memory

The agent supports conversation history:

- Maintains previous context
- Uses history only as conversational memory
- Optimizes prompts when history grows
- Reduces unnecessary token usage


### ⚙️ Smart Prompt Management

The project includes a dynamic prompt builder:

- Detailed prompts for new conversations
- Optimized prompts for long conversations
- Intent-based response handling:
  - Price
  - Time
  - Trend
  - Analysis
  - Forecast
  - Buy/Sell


### 🔄 API-Based Architecture

Designed with modular data providers:

- Market data sources
- News providers
- AI model integration
- Future API fallback support

## Agent Capabilities

Example questions:

You:
Based on today's news, how will the gold price change?

Agent:
Based on today's news, the gold price is likely to experience continued downward pressure due to several key factors:

1. **Current Market Direction**: The market is currently bearish, with gold prices falling below critical support levels. The strengthening U.S. dollar is making gold less affordable for international buyers, contributing to the decline.

2. **Bullish Factors**:
   - Geopolitical tensions, particularly related to the U.S.-Iran peace talks and ongoing tensions in the Middle East, could lead to a sudden increase in demand for gold as a safe-haven asset if conflicts escalate.
   - Unexpected economic data that suggests higher inflation could temporarily boost gold prices as investors seek a hedge against rising costs.

3. **Bearish Factors**:
   - The strong U.S. dollar, which is at a one-year high, continues to diminish gold's appeal.
   - The Federal Reserve's hawkish stance and expectations of multiple interest rate hikes (67% chance in September and 88% in December) are likely to keep gold prices under pressure as investors shift to interest-bearing assets.
   - Recent declines in oil prices, despite raising inflation concerns, are overshadowed by the Fed's tightening policy, further limiting gold's attractiveness.

4. **Important Events**: Upcoming inflation data releases will be critical in shaping market sentiment and could influence the Fed's policy decisions, thereby impacting gold prices.

In summary, while there may be short-term fluctuations due to geopolitical developments, the overall sentiment suggests that gold prices are likely to remain under pressure in the near term.



You:
What is the gold price forecast for next month?

Agent:
**Gold Price Forecast for Next Month:**

**Expected Market Direction:** Bearish

**Bullish Factors:**
1. **Geopolitical Tensions:** Ongoing conflicts, particularly in the Middle East, could lead to increased demand for gold as a safe-haven asset if tensions escalate.
2. **Potential Inflation Indicators:** If inflation data shows unexpected increases, it may lead to a more cautious approach from the Fed, which could support gold prices.

**Bearish Factors:**
1. **Strong U.S. Dollar:** Continued strength in the dollar due to expectations of Fed interest rate hikes makes gold more expensive for international buyers, suppressing demand.
2. **Fed's Tightening Monetary Policy:** A high probability of further interest rate increases in the coming months could further diminish gold's appeal as an investment, leading to lower prices.

**Important Events:**
1. **U.S. Economic Data Releases:** Key inflation indicators, particularly the Personal Consumption Expenditures data, will be crucial in shaping Fed policy and could impact gold prices.
2. **Fed Meetings:** The outcomes of upcoming Federal Reserve meetings and any announcements regarding interest rate policies will be pivotal.

In summary, while there are some factors that could potentially support gold prices, the prevailing conditions suggest a bearish outlook for next month, primarily driven by the strong dollar and the Fed's anticipated rate hikes.
